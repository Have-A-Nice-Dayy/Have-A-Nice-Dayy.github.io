Overview

I am currently working on making an 8 bit CPU. After I took an ET 182 course at NMSU, I felt motivation to keep working on mechatronics. I looked up a lot of in depth guides on how CPU's work, on my search I found a man by the name of Ben Eater who focuses a lot on mechatronics. Ben Eater talks a lot about the work from Alan Turing. I find mechatronics very interesting so I took on the challenge of creating an 8 bit CPU based off of Ben Eater's 8 bit CPU.

Currently, all of the games I plan on developing have a story board but the only game I am currently working on is a remaster of Thanks Tanks. For the rest of my projects, I will continue to document them and when I feel it is polished enough I will post them to this website under the appropiate project. College and work are currently taking up a lot of my time, so during my free time I will work on these projects as much as I can.
